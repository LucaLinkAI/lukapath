### Year Pillar
```
stem_index  = (year - 4) % 10
branch_index = (year - 4) % 12
```
Example: 1987 → stem (1983%10=3) → 丁(3), branch (1983%12=3) → 卯(3)...

### Month Pillar
Solar term approximate start dates (day of month):
- 寅月(月序1): Feb ~4 | 卯月(2): Mar ~6 | 辰月(3): Apr ~5
- 巳月(4): May ~6 | 午月(5): Jun ~6 | 未月(6): Jul ~7
- 申月(7): Aug ~7 | 酉月(8): Sep ~8 | 戌月(9): Oct ~8
- 亥月(10): Nov ~7 | 子月(11): Dec ~7 | 丑月(12): Jan ~6 (next year)

Month branch mapping (month sequence 1–12 → branch index):
月序: 1→寅(2), 2→卯(3), 3→辰(4), 4→巳(5), 5→午(6), 6→未(7), 7→申(8), 8→酉(9), 9→戌(10), 10→亥(11), 11→子(0), 12→丑(1)

Month stem: base = ((year_stem_index % 5) * 2 + 2) % 10, then add (month_seq - 1) % 10
- Year stem 甲/己 → month 寅 starts at 丙(2)
- Year stem 乙/庚 → month 寅 starts at 戊(4)
- Year stem 丙/辛 → month 寅 starts at 庚(6)
- Year stem 丁/壬 → month 寅 starts at 壬(8)
- Year stem 戊/癸 → month 寅 starts at 甲(0)

If birth date is before the solar term for that month (e.g., born Feb 2 = still 丑月), use the prior month. If born in January before ~Jan 6, use prior year's 丑月.

### Day Pillar
Calculate Julian Day Number (JDN):
```
a  = floor((14 - month) / 12)
yy = year + 4800 - a
mm = month + 12*a - 3
JDN = day + floor((153*mm + 2)/5) + 365*yy + floor(yy/4) - floor(yy/100) + floor(yy/400) - 32045

stem_index  = (JDN + 9) % 10
branch_index = (JDN + 1) % 12
```
Example: 1987-01-13 → JDN=2446808, stem=(2446808+9)%10=7→辛, branch=(2446808+1)%12=9→酉...

a=1, yy=6586, mm=10, JDN=13+floor(1532/5)+365*6586+floor(6586/4)-floor(6586/100)+floor(6586/400)-32045
= 13+306+2403890+1646-65+16-32045 = 2373761? 
The exact JDN computation is precise — trust the formula. If you get an unexpected result, double-check the arithmetic carefully.

### Hour Pillar
```
branch_index = (hour == 23) ? 0 : floor((hour + 1) / 2) % 12
stem_index   = (day_stem_index % 5 * 2 + branch_index) % 10
```

### 格局 Pattern (simplified)
The month branch's primary hidden stem (藏干主气) vs. Day Master:
- Hidden stem = Day Master: 比肩/劫财格 (strong self)
- Hidden stem generates Day Master: 印格 (正印 or 偏印)
- Hidden stem is generated by Day Master: 食神/伤官格
- Hidden stem controls Day Master: 官格 (正官 or 七杀)  
- Day Master controls hidden stem: 财格 (正财 or 偏财)

正 = same polarity (both yin or both yang), 偏 = different polarity.

### 大限 (10-Year Major Cycles)
Starting age = 局数 number (e.g., 火六局 starts at age 6). Each cycle = 10 years.
Cycles flow through palaces counter-clockwise (for males in yang year birth / females in yin year birth) or clockwise (opposite).

Male born in yang year (甲丙戊庚壬) or Female born in yin year (乙丁己辛癸): cycles go counter-clockwise from 命宫.
Male born in yin year or Female born in yang year: cycles go clockwise.

Label each 大限 with the palace name and its key stars. Identify which palace the person is in NOW (current age), the one before, and the one after.
